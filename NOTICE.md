## Inspiration And Asset Policy

Grenzmark is inspired by classic settlement strategy games, especially the
node/road economy style of Die Siedler II, Return to the Roots, and Widelands.

No original commercial game assets are included. Please do not contribute copied
sprites, sounds, maps, or other copyrighted files from commercial games.

Widelands assets have their own licenses. If any Widelands-derived asset is ever
added, it must include the required license and attribution next to the file.

## Included Project Assets

The current project-local sprites, terrain textures, and icons are original or
generated for this repository and are released under the project MIT License
unless a file says otherwise.
